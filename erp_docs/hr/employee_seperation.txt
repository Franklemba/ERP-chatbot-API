Training Guide: Employee Separation Clearance

- Go to HR > Employee Separation
- Select Employee who is exiting
- Enter Separation Reason (e.g. resignation, termination, retirement)
- Set Last Working Day
- Initiate Clearance Process
- Clearance tasks are auto-assigned to relevant departments (IT, Finance, Admin, etc.)
- Each department marks their clearance as complete
- HR reviews all department clearances
- Final Approval is submitted by HR
- Exit Interview can be scheduled if required
